1. Primary Auxiliary Verbs

Digunakan untuk: membentuk struktur tenses, pertanyaan, atau kalimat negatif.

a. To be → am, is, are, was, were
   Digunakan di Continuous tenses dan Passive voice
   Contoh: She is studying. / The book was written by him.

b. Do / Does / Did
   Digunakan untuk membentuk pertanyaan & negasi pada Simple Present dan Simple Past
   Contoh:
    - Do you like tea?
    - He doesn’t go to school.
    - Did they come yesterday?
   ⚠️ Catatan: "Do/does/did" tidak punya makna sendiri — hanya alat bantu.

c. Have / Has / Had
   Digunakan sebagai auxiliary dalam Perfect tenses
   Contoh:
    - I have finished my work. (Present Perfect)
    - She had left before I arrived. (Past Perfect)
