Kelompok Verbs (Kata Kerja)
6

Consider: Pertimbangkan (Please consider my offer)

Retain/Remain: Menahan/Tetap (Remain calm)
Encountered: Menjumpai (Encountered an enemy)

Kelompok Adverbs (Kata Keterangan)
7

Barely: Nyaris tidak (I barely escaped)

Exactly: Tepat sekali (That's exactly right)
Truly: Benar-benar (A truly rare treasure)
Entirely: Sepenuhnya (I entirely agree)
Indeed: Memang/Tentu (It is indeed strange)

Concerned: Khawatir (We are concerned for you)
